1. Marriage and Divorce

We handle cases under Hindu, Muslim, Christian, Sikh, and Parsi laws.

Essential Conditions:
  • Consent & Mental capacity of both parties.
  • Legal Age: Male (21), Female (18).
Grounds for Divorce:
  • Mutual Consent
  • Cruelty, Violence, or Adultery
  • Desertion or Mental Disorder

Types of Custody:
  • Sole Custody
  • Joint Custody
  • Shared Custody

Determined by: Consent of parents, age, and mental capacity of the child.

Options for Resolving Family Disputes

We offer multiple avenues to resolve conflicts efficiently:

  • Mediation: Amicable resolution through a neutral third party.
  • Family Counseling: Professional counseling to bridge gaps.
  • Arbitration: Binding decision by a neutral party.
  • Court Litigation: Filing cases in Family Court when necessary.

Key Legal Terms

  • Marriage: Legal and social union between two people.
  • Divorce: The legal process of ending a marriage.
  • Custody: Legal care and guardianship of a child.
  • Inheritance: Transfer of property through a will or law.
  • Dowry: Property/money given to the bride's family (Prohibited by law).
  • Domestic Violence: Violence or abuse within the home.
