Is TDS applicable on sale of property by NRI in India?

Yes. TDS is mandatory on property sale by NRIs under the Income Tax Act. The rate depends on whether the capital gain is long-term or short-term. Proper calculation, TDS deduction, deposit and Form 26QB compliance are essential to avoid penalties.

Do NRIs need GPA or SPA for property transactions in India?

Yes. NRIs commonly execute General Power of Attorney (GPA) or Special Power of Attorney (SPA) to authorise property sale, purchase or registration in India. The POA must be executed abroad and adjudicated or registered in India to become legally valid.

Is adjudication or registration of Power of Attorney mandatory?

Yes. A Power of Attorney executed outside India must be adjudicated or stamped in India within the prescribed time. In certain transactions, registration of GPA or SPA is mandatory to ensure full legal validity and acceptance by authorities.

Can NRIs obtain succession certificate, mutation and title transfer in India?

Yes. NRIs can apply for succession certificate, legal heir certificate, mutation of property records and transfer of title through authorised legal representatives in India without personal presence, subject to proper documentation and court procedures.
