Latest Marriage Registration Rules in Uttar Pradesh

As per the latest government guidelines, marriage registration can only be done in the district where either the bride or groom is a resident.

Valid address proof such as Aadhaar Card, Passport or Voter ID is mandatory for verification. For Arya Samaj or temple marriages, parents of both parties must be present as witnesses.

Marriage photographs and video recording must be submitted in a pen drive at the registrar office.
