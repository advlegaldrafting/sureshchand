Important Changes in Marriage Registration Rules in Uttar Pradesh

The Government of Uttar Pradesh has introduced significant changes in the marriage registration process. As per the new rules, marriages solemnized in any other district or in any other state can no longer be registered in a different district.

Now, for marriage registration in a particular district, it is mandatory that either the bride or the groom must have a residential address in that district. One of the following address proofs must be submitted at the Registrar Office: Aadhaar Card, Passport, or Voter ID.

In cases where the marriage has been solemnized at an Arya Samaj Mandir or any other temple, the parents of the bride and groom must be present at the Marriage Registrar Office as witnesses.

Additionally, clear photographs of the marriage ceremony along with the marriage video must be saved in a pen drive and submitted to the Marriage Registrar Office at the time of registration.

Note: Rules and procedures are subject to change from time to time. It is strongly advised to consult a legal expert before proceeding with marriage registration.
